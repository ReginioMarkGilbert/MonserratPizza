Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our
ingredients, ordering process, and delivery options.

What are your ingredients?

Our ingredients are sourced locally and imported from Italy to ensure the authentic taste of
our pizzas.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website, mobile app, or by calling our hotline. We offer
both delivery and pickup options.

What are your delivery options?

We offer free delivery within a 5-kilometer radius. For areas outside the radius, a delivery fee
may apply.

Can I customize my pizza?

Yes, you can customize your pizza by choosing your preferred toppings and crust type.
Additional charges may apply for certain toppings.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, credit card, and online payment methods such as GCash and PayPal.
